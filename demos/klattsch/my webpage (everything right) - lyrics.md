# my webpage (everything right) - lyrics, transcription, and translation

A pikopop / shibuya-kei number for klattsch + Game Boy, sung by a bright girlish
voice (formant shift 1.5x plus tilt). English and Japanese are interwoven line to
line and sometimes within a line, in the manner of Shibuya-kei groups like YMCK,
Plus-Tech Squeeze Box, and Fantastic Plastic Machine.

Japanese is given in romaji with the original kana and an English gloss. The
`voice` is stylized and mora-timed to the beat, so treat this as the intended
libretto rather than a phonetic transcript.

Tempo 180 BPM with a swung eighth groove, 48 seconds, one pass (no loop): intro through outro.

## chorus A

| line | reading | meaning |
|------|---------|---------|
| i did everything right! | (English) | |
| zenbu umaku itta! | ぜんぶ うまく いった！ | It all went perfectly! |

The two chorus lines mirror each other: the English claim and its Japanese echo.

## bridge

Wordless melodic runs (the lead's showcase), an a-i-u-e-o vowel ladder, then:

| line | reading | meaning |
|------|---------|---------|
| yatta! | やった！ | I did it! / Hooray! |

## final chorus

Chorus A and B repeat, chorus A now doubled by a harmony voice a sixth up.

## complete Japanese, in order

1. konnichiwa - こんにちは - hello
2. atarashii - あたらしい - new
3. mainichi - まいにち - every day
4. kimi to - きみと - with you
5. intanet - インターネット - internet (Japanese pronunciation)
6. ne - ね - (sentence-final particle: "right?", "you know")
7. wakuwaku - わくわく - excited, thrilled (mimetic)
8. dokidoki - どきどき - heart-pounding (mimetic)
9. daisuki - だいすき - love (it) / like (it) very much
10. motto - もっと - more
11. zenbu - ぜんぶ - everything, all
12. umaku itta - うまく いった - went well, succeeded
13. kirakira - きらきら - glittering, sparkling (mimetic)
14. no - の - (possessive/attributive particle)
15. sekai - せかい - world
16. wa - は - (topic particle)
17. a-i-u-e-o - あいうえお - the five Japanese vowels (sung as a run)
18. yatta - やった - I did it!, hooray!
19. arigatou - ありがとう - thank you
20. mata ne - またね - see you later
